Literary Events

Useful Details

The literary events conducted at Aagaaz aims to bring out the talent of the budding engineers and to build up their confidence to express their ideas. The events are conducted both in Hindi and English. Students participate with a fast flowing idea along with proficiency to express to bring out their views, their thoughts that capture their imagination.
Its time to speak, time to put forward our ideas…Let’s be more expressive!
Debate
For the first time in JEC we are having student's Debate Society by their effort we will have Debate competition.
In this competitive world we live, let’s learn the art of persuasion, the art of sharing knowledge through interactive argument, the art to put forward your point, the art to listen, the art to answer…. and there you are the winner!

Extempore
Just 2 minutes to think and then you are on the roller coaster of your knowledge, your ideas. The participants are to speak on the current burning topics given. Cogwheels are turning, sparks are being ignited are new ideas are being created everyday…
Buck up your knowledge on the current topic, recollect your thoughts and there you are on the dais. The thought process and proficiency are to be judged on.

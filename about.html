About Us

Welcome to aaGaaZ15. We are here to create an environment of fun & excitement while
learning new skills in different
areas of Arts, Music, Sports, Science, Technology & various
other emerging fields. Along with conducting intra-society Competitions, we are also working hard to offer .
quality training sessions for people of various branches.

Our Goal is to arrange competitions, in our own societies to
help you save your valuable Time, Money & to
make it very convenient for us & our family to
be part of it so that everyone can get maximum benefit from these events.

Our Vision is to create a local platform (with standards) for grooming the talent & skills of
students from all age groups in Arts, Music, Dance, Sports, Science, Technology & many other exciting & emerging fields / areas.

This event is done under the guidance of Director of Physical Education Rajendra Singh and by
the willingness of Pricipal Dr. Pradeep Kumar Jhinge.
